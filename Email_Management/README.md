# Email Management System

## 📧 Overview
This system helps organize and manage emails for Gift Zakeyo's education and business activities.

## 🗂️ Structure

### Templates/
- Reusable email templates for common situations
- Customizable for different recipients

### Drafts/
- Email drafts before sending
- Version control for important emails

### Sent_Records/
- Records of important sent emails
- Tracking and follow-up reminders

### Strategies/
- Email sending strategies
- Timing and follow-up schedules

## 🔒 Security Notes
- **Never store passwords** or login credentials
- **Do not include sensitive information** in templates
- **Use placeholders** for personal details
- **Actual email sending** happens through Gmail interface

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. Use templates for common email types
2. Customize with specific details
3. Copy to Gmail for sending
4. Record important sent emails

## 📞 Contact Management
- Maintain contact lists separately
- Update regularly
- Note important details about contacts
