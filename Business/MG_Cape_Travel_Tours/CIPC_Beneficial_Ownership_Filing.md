## 1. What is Beneficial Ownership?

A **beneficial owner** is the natural person who ultimately owns or controls a company. Even if a company is registered in someone else's name, the "real owner" must be declared to CIPC.

Since Gift Zakeyo is the **sole director, sole incorporator, and sole shareholder** of MG CAPE TRAVEL AND TOURS (PTY) LTD, Gift is the **beneficial owner**.

## 6. Ongoing Obligations

After initial filing, you must update Beneficial Ownership whenever:
- A new beneficial owner is added
- An existing beneficial owner's details change
- The nature of ownership changes

Updates must be filed within **10 business days** of the change.

Also required at **Annual Return** filing each year.
