| 5 | Total energy supplied per coulomb of charge by the cell; measured in volts when no current flows | 2 |
| 6.1 | ε = I(R + r) → 9 = I(4 + 0.5) → 9 = 4.5I → I = 2 A | 3 |
| 6.2 | V = ε - Ir = 9 - (2×0.5) = 9 - 1 = 8 V | 3 |
| 7 | ε = V + Ir → 1.5 = 1.2 + 0.3r → 0.3 = 0.3r → r = 1 Ω | 4 |
| 8 | Starter motor draws large current → large Ir drop → terminal voltage drops → headlight dims | 3 |
