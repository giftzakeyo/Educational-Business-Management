# Electric Circuits - 6-Week Lesson Plan (Grade 12 IEB)

## Overview
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Subject** | IEB Physical Sciences - Physics |
| **Grade** | 12 |
| **Term** | 3 |
| **Topic** | Electric Circuits |
| **Duration** | 6 weeks (2 lessons per week) |
| **Prior Knowledge** | Grade 11 Ohm's Law, basic circuits |

---

## Week 1: Ohm's Law Revision
| Lesson | Topic | Activities |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 1.1 | Ohm's Law & Resistance | V = I × R calculations, factors affecting resistance |
| 1.2 | Ohm's Law Experiment | Circuit diagrams, measuring V and I, plotting V-I graph |

**Homework:** Worksheet 1 (Circuit Basics)

---

## Week 2: Internal Resistance
| Lesson | Topic | Activities |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 2.1 | Emf vs Terminal Voltage | Definition of emf, ε = I(R + r) derivation |
| 2.2 | Internal Resistance Calculations | ε = V + Ir problems, measuring internal resistance |

**Homework:** Internal resistance problem set

---

## Week 3: Series and Parallel Circuits
| Lesson | Topic | Activities |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 3.1 | Series Circuits | Total resistance, voltage dividers, current |
| 3.2 | Parallel Circuits | Total resistance formula, current dividers |

**Homework:** Worksheet 2 (Circuit Calculations)

---

## Week 4: Combination Circuits
| Lesson | Topic | Activities |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 4.1 | Series-Parallel Circuits | Step-by-step simplification |
| 4.2 | Complex Circuit Problems | Finding I, V, R in all parts of circuit |

**Homework:** Combination circuit problems

---

## Week 5: Power & Energy
| Lesson | Topic | Activities |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 5.1 | Electrical Power | P = VI = I²R = V²/R |
| 5.2 | Energy & Cost | E = Pt, kWh, electricity tariffs |

**Homework:** Energy cost calculations

---

## Week 6: Revision & Assessment
| Lesson | Topic | Activities |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 6.1 | Past Paper Practice | IEB past exam questions |
| 6.2 | Unit Test | 40-mark assessment |

---
