# Optical Phenomena - Grade 12 IEB Physics (Term 3)

## IEB Curriculum Alignment

### Topics Covered:
1. **Photoelectric Effect** — Einstein's photon theory, work function, threshold frequency
2. **Emission and Absorption Spectra** — Line spectra, energy levels
3. **Dual Nature of Light** — Wave-particle duality

### Key Equations:
- E = hf (energy of a photon)
- E = hc/λ
- W₀ = hf₀ (work function)
- E_k(max) = hf - W₀ = hf - hf₀ (Einstein's photoelectric equation)
- E_k(max) = ½mv² (maximum kinetic energy of ejected electron)

### Constants:
- Planck's constant: h = 6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s
- Speed of light: c = 3 × 10⁸ m·s⁻¹
- Elementary charge: e = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C
- Electron mass: m_e = 9.11 × 10⁻³¹ kg
- 1 eV = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ J

### IEB Exam Weighting:
- Usually **10-15 marks** in Paper 1
- High-frequency: Photoelectric effect calculations, graph interpretation
- Often includes a diagram of the photoelectric effect experiment
