## **🛠️ Required Resources:**

### **Essential Equipment:**
1. Ripple tank with accessories
2. Tuning forks set
3. Slinky springs (for transverse and longitudinal)
4. Laser pointer with diffraction grating
5. Sound generator and speakers
6. Basic measuring equipment (rulers, stopwatches)

### **Minimum Viable Setup:**
- String and masses for wave speed experiments
- Tuning fork and water for sound demonstrations
- Prism or CD for light diffraction
- Calculator for all students

### **Digital Alternatives:**
- PhET Interactive Simulations (free)
- Online oscilloscope simulations
- Video demonstrations
- Virtual labs
