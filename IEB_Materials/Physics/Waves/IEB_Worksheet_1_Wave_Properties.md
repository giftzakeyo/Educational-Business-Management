### **Section B: Definitions (5 marks)**
1. **Wave:** Disturbance that transfers energy without transferring matter (1)
2. **Amplitude:** Maximum displacement from equilibrium position (1)
3. **Frequency:** Number of complete waves passing a point per second (1)
4. **Transverse wave:** Wave where particles vibrate perpendicular to wave direction (1)
5. **Period:** Time for one complete wave to pass a point (1)
