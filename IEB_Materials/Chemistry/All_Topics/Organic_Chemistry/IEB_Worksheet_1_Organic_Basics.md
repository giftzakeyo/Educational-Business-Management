### **Section B: Definitions (5 marks)**
1. **Homologous series:** Family of compounds with same functional group, general formula, gradual property change (1)
2. **Functional group:** Reactive part of molecule that determines properties (1)
3. **Structural isomer:** Same molecular formula, different structural arrangement (1)
4. **Polymer:** Large molecule made of repeating monomer units (1)
5. **Hydrocarbon:** Compound containing only carbon and hydrogen (1)
