### **Section B: Definitions (5 marks)**
1. **Dynamic equilibrium:** Forward rate = reverse rate, concentrations constant, microscopic activity continues (1)
2. **Equilibrium constant:** Ratio of product to reactant concentrations at equilibrium (1)
3. **Le Chatelier's principle:** System shifts to counteract disturbance (1)
4. **Reaction quotient:** Same as Kc formula but for any concentrations, not just equilibrium (1)
5. **Homogeneous equilibrium:** All species in same phase (1)

### **Section C: Calculations (10 marks)**

2. [NO₂]² = Kc×[N₂O₄] = 0.36×0.45 = 0.162, [NO₂] = √0.162 = 0.402 M (2)
3. x = 0.2, [A] = 0.8 M, Kc = (0.4)²/0.8 = 0.16/0.8 = 0.2 (2)
